Isaac Asimov’s Three Laws of Robotics

    Isaac Asimov’s Three Laws of Robotics were created to address the negative concerns of robots:

  1.   A robot may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm.
  2.   A robot must obey orders given to it by human beings except where such orders would conflict with the First Law.
  3.   A robot must protect its own existence as long as such protection does not conflict with the First or Second Law.

Company Name and Purpose

Avalon

We build AI Robots to promote education and business.

What is Avalon?

Avalon is a learning and training platform that recommends a custom curriculum based on a myriad of personalized questions and, if applicable, previously taken courses. It uses a recurrent neural network (RNN) for natural language processing (NLP). It also uses a convolutional neural network (CNN) for course classification and an RNN for the recommendation system. This allows for courses to be automatically managed and categorized by the artificial intelligence. Avalon would act as a teaching assistant, working alongside teachers and trainers to individually optimize education for students. Avalon is an egg shaped robot that travels on wheels. Avalon is equipped with cameras, microphones, speakers, and a wifi adaptor. It connects to the host’s network so it can easily communicate with many students at the same time.

Applications

  • Education:

    • Avalon recommends classes which will keep students engaged. In addition, the course platform prevents students from being distracted by other sites.

  • Business:

    • Avalon can be used to train new employees for job specific work, therefore the company will not need to waste resources and time having another employee train them.

  • Recreational:

    • Avalon’s custom course recommendations make learning fun for anyone, making it easy for people to learn in their free time.

  • Research:

    • The data gathered by avalon can be used in research for studies regarding popular interests, as well as what subjects different groups of people are interested in.
